Paper Backup Forms

Fillable PDFs for field staff to use when eMR is down or throwing errors on a visit. Fill out, save, and upload the visit into the EMR once it's back up.

Only use these when eMR isn't working. These are backups for documentation continuity — not a replacement for normal charting. Once eMR is available again, enter the visit and attach or reference this form per your usual documentation process.

How to use

  1. Tap Download and open the PDF on your phone, tablet, or laptop.
  2. Fill in the fields directly in the PDF — no printing needed.
  3. Save the completed PDF with the patient's name and visit date in the filename.
  4. As soon as eMR is back up, complete the visit note there and keep this PDF as backup documentation per policy.
  5. If you're not sure where to save the completed PDF, check with your supervisor.
